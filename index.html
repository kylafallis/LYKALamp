III. The Integrated Solution

The LYKA Lamp converts organic waste directly into stable, usable electricity using a **Microbial Fuel Cell (MFC)**. This is an end-to-end decentralized energy solution, engineered to maximize microbial output and provide genuine functional light.

**$16.77** Total Material Cost

**2.43 hrs** Continuous Run Time (9V LED)

System Architecture

The lamp is built with **3D-printed ABA filament** housing and features a custom circuit including the **LM2596 Buck Power Convertor** and **TP4056 Charger Module** for efficient energy storage.
