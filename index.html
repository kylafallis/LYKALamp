III. Description of Invention [cite: 76]

The **LYKA Lamp** is a revolutionary smart desk lamp engineered to combat **digital eye strain** and regulate natural **sleep cycles** by proactively monitoring the user's environment and activity. Unlike standard smart lamps, LYKA utilizes a proprietary sensor suite to measure both ambient room light and the user's proximity/screen time[cite: 76].

When the system detects prolonged periods of focus or evening hours, it **automatically and seamlessly transitions** the light's color temperature from a cool, focus-enhancing white light to a **warm, melatonin-friendly amber glow**[cite: 76]. This gentle "digital sunset" signals to the user that it's time to take a break or wind down, reducing exposure to disruptive blue light spectrums[cite: 80, 81].

It is an **original idea** and a significant advancement over existing lighting products because of its adaptive, biological-rhythm-aware automation[cite: 69].

**100-Word Summary:** The LYKA Lamp is a novel smart desk lamp designed to actively monitor ambient light and user screen-time exposure. It automatically adjusts its light color and intensity to support the user's circadian rhythm. By gently shifting to a warm, amber spectrum during prolonged use or in the evening, the lamp signals a natural break and reduces blue light impact. This non-intrusive, proactive solution directly addresses digital eye strain and promotes healthier sleep patterns for students and professionals.
